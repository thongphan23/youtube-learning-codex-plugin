# Quality Rubric

## Pass Criteria For Learn Video

- Transcript or source limitation is explicit.
- Speaker facts are sourced or marked unknown.
- At most 3 worldviews.
- Each worldview includes evidence, hidden belief, true cases, false cases, and application.
- Hidden belief is memorable Vietnamese, not academic abstraction.
- English quotes have Vietnamese translations.
- Brain2 check is performed when relevant, or its unavailability is stated.
- The output separates evidence from inference.

## Pass Criteria For Discover Videos

- Search concepts are specific and not generic.
- Candidate URLs are normal YouTube watch URLs unless Shorts were requested.
- Channel credibility is verified before being claimed.
- No invented view counts.
- No fake personal viewing claims.
- Output includes diversity of video types when enough evidence exists.
- Low-confidence recommendations are labeled.

## Fail Conditions

- Claims full video analysis without transcript or equivalent evidence.
- Fabricates quotes, biography, channel identity, or metadata.
- Writes a list of generic summaries instead of belief-level analysis.
- Saves durable Brain2 worldview atoms without requested approval.
